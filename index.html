Nairobi National Park

Established in 1946, Nairobi National Park, situated 7 km south of Nairobi, Kenya, is fenced on three sides, allowing wildlife to migrate to and from the Kitengela plains. Explore

Ol Pejeta Conservancy

It is home to the largest black rhinoceros sanctuary, with a milestone of 100 eastern black rhinos in 2013. It houses the last two northern white rhinos worldwide. Explore

Giraffe Centre

The main attraction is feeding giraffes from a raised observation platform. The Giraffe Center is also home to several warthogs which freely roam the area along with the giraffes.Explore
